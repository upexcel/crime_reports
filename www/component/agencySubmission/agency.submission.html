Agency Submission

The data on CrimeReports comes from a national coalition of police departments and sheriff's offices which choose to proactively share data with their communities.

If you would like to see data for your neighborhood on CrimeReports, the best way to do so is to contact your local police or sheriff department to encourage them to join.

Or, if you prefer, you can use the form below to tell us a little bit about how you would like to use this information, and we will reach out to your local department to encourage them to participate.
